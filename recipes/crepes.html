Description

Bring Paris to your kitchen with these delicious berries & cream filled crepes. For presentation and extra deliciousness, add chocolate syrup on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup 2% Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 1/4 Cup Melted Butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups Mixed Berries of Your Choice
  • Chocolate Drizzle, as Desired

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine flour, sugar, milk, water, melted butter, and eggs. Whisk the mixture until smooth, cover, and refrigerate for 30 minutes. Lightly coat an 8-inch nonstick skillet with additional butter or nonstick spray; head over medium heat.
  2. Spoon slightly less than 3 tablespoons of batter in the center of the pan; tilt and rotate to cover the entire bottom of the pan (6 inches). Cook until the top is dry and the edges are starting to brown.
  3. Loosen the edges with a rubber spatula. Flip the crepe over to brown on the other side. Carefully slide the scraper under the crepe and remove to a clean plate. Repeat until batter is used (~16 crepes).
  4. Stack crepes within squares of waxed paper between layers.
  5. To serve the crepes, spoon about 1/4 cup berries in each crepe and fold. Top with chocolate drizzle as desired and enjoy!
